Marketing Technology Coordinator

Requirements

Employment Type

FullTime

Date Posted

October 4, 2021

Job Location

Atlanta -USA

Job Description

We are looking for someone with impeccable research skills and is into pursuing new business leads for the growth of our business. The role involves active coordination across teams and demands exceptional interpersonal skills. The executive is expected to work on new business proposals and presentations which are a persuasive representation of Centre Source services.

Responsibilities
  • Meeting the quotas in terms of calls, meetings, and exceeding the assigned sales goals periodically.
  • Building business relationships with current and potential clients.
  • Lead Generation/constant interaction with the clients/prospecting clients from across various countries
  • Make cold calls for new business leads using a defined script
  • Actively follow up with clients through mail and calls to achieve the assigned task.
  • Conduct Mail campaigns regularly for those who are not in the pipeline.
  • Maintain the database of prospect clients in the corresponding tools.
  • Research on target prospects using a variety of sales tools.
  • Actively engage in social networking platforms like LinkedIn and Sales Navigator.
  • Collaborating with the sales and leadership team to secure, retain, and grow business accounts
  • Collaborates with the team for strategy development for different accounts.
  • Set appointments with the prospects using available channels.
  • Responsible for individual KPI’s
  • Use available tools to compile weekly, monthly, quarterly and yearly reports.
Experience

We are looking for someone with impeccable research skills and is into pursuing new business leads for the growth of our business. The role involves active coordination across teams and demands exceptional interpersonal skills.

Experience
  • BTech/MBA with 1-3 Years of experience in International Sales,\.
  • Good understanding of the current Digital trends, jargon etc to hold meaningful technical conversations
  • Good logical reasoning and analytical skills.
  • Able to work in a team as well as an individual contributor
  • Good at multitasking, time management, and prioritizing
  • Exceptional communication skills– both written and verbal
  • Self-starter and goal-oriented
  • High energy, positive attitude
  • Need to proactively work across international time zones to drive sales.
