Why Asian Media Group?

Operating in the UK, US and India, our reach extends across the globe. Our websites attract over a million unique visitors every month and our business and consumer publications reach over 1.3 million readers each month.

Our business began on 1st April 1968 with the launch of our flagship title, Garavi Gujarat. Today, AMG is a diversified media house, publishing some of the country’s most iconic and trusted Asian media brands and hosting some of the UK’s leading events.
